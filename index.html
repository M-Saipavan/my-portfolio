Supply-Side Energy Management of a Commercial Facility with Renewable Energy Sources

Technology: Economic analysis methods such as Payback method, Cost Savings etc…,

Software Used: MATLAB

Description: Economic analysis methods are widely used for energy audits to determine if an identified energy-conservation measure is financially feasible. By considering payback method and cost savings methods, one can calculate how much of rate of return will be possible under the identified energy-conservation measure and how much cost savings can be made after implementation.

In this project an education building is considered and solar and wind energy resources are considered as conservation measures presenting payback period, cost savings and other advantages of using renewable resources including with grid supply.

Novel Maximum Power Point Tracking Technique

Technology: Perturb and Observe Methods and Fuzzy Logic.

Software Used: MATLAB Simulink.

Description: Perturb and Observe (P&O) method is widely used for MPPT due to its simple implementation and low cost. Fuzzy Logic (FL) is another general technique that achieves vastly improved performance of MPPT technique in terms of response speed and low fluctuation. In this Project a novel MPPT technique based on Fuzzy Logic Control and P&O Method is Presented incorporates the advantages of P&O-MPPT and FL MPPT.
